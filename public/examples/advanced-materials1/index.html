This sample shows ATON support for new GLTF extensions related to advanced materials.

In this case we create a simple ATON node and load a remote 3D model (GLTF) of a dragon by Khronos, showcasing extensions:
KHR_materials_transmission,
KHR_materials_volume,
KHR_materials_variants.


In addition to light probing and usual lighting, you can see real-time refraction of dragon 3D model through the checkboard, also casting real-time shadows.
